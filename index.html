Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study. You will be acting as a customer service agent who is assigned a task and will use a conversational agent to resolve the issue. Please follow these steps to complete your participation:

  1. Access the Survey:
    • When you are done reading this info press the start button at the bottom of this page to access the survey form.
  2. Interact with the Conversational Agent:
    • Within the survey, you will find a link to a conversational agent.
    • Click on the link and interact with the conversational agent to resolve the case.
  3. Return to the Survey:
    • After interacting with the conversational agent, return to the survey form.
  4. Complete the Survey:
    • Proceed to the last page of the survey and answer all the remaining questions.
    • Once you have submitted the form you can choose to participate in a follow-up study to this experiment.

Please make sure to complete both the interaction with the conversational agent and the survey to ensure your participation is fully recorded. Thank you for your time and cooperation!
