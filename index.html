Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study. Please follow these steps to complete your participation:

  1. Access the Survey:
    • Press Start to access the survey form.
  2. Interact with the Virtual Agent:
    • Within the survey, you will find a link to a virtual assistant.
    • Click on the link and interact with the virtual agent to receive information about different BMW models.
    • Based on the information provided, select the BMW model that best fits your preferences.
  3. Return to the Survey:
    • After interacting with the virtual agent, return to the survey form.
    • Indicate the BMW model you selected in the appropriate section of the form.
  4. Complete the Survey:
    • Proceed to the last page of the survey and answer all the remaining questions.
    • Once you have submitted the form you can close all windows.
